COMMUNITY

By shopping locally, you support the small businesses that are the lifeblood of your community.

GROWTH

We make it possible for small business to grow their brands and customer base all on one platform.

CONNECTIONS

By providing an all in one platform businesses are able to be more connected to their communities as well as other businesses.

Mission Statement

To help small clothing stores exclusively profit on a free and marketable platform where they can gain a stron foothold in thier communities.

Vision Statement

To equalize the playing gield between small and large businesses while promoting sustainable and ethically sourced products.

Platform Features Promise

Our platform offers a variety of different resources that users are able to navigate around which will help our customers find the right small business for them. On the directory page we have a list of all of the small businesses on our platform. We used the Google Maps API to embed all of the businesses close to you on our find page. In addition to that, we have a blog page where we feature a different small business each month and offer a coupon valid through that month.

Purpose And Inspiration

This website was created while keeping the needs of small business and their customers in mind. We created a win win situation that will be benificial to both sides. Small businesses have always had a lot of compitition from larger businesses but during these difficult times even more so. We hope to allow our customers to shop for more unique pieces while also keeping their community businesses open.

Small businesses are vital to the survival of communities and families. Many people don't recognize their importance and the key role they place. They provide local opportunities and alternatives to people who are commited to helping thier communities trive. These businesses also act as centers of safty and public gatherings.

Marketable Growth Platform

This reduces the chances smaller businesses have of rising up against the competition in order to grow. As this website is focused on small businesses specifically, there is an environment where there are opportunities for all businesses to grow regardless of their size.
