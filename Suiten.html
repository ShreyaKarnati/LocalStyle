Suiten Tie Co.

This is our local men’s wear shop which brings the latest styles with a fresh twist. The owner, Marcus Rayalt has more to say on where he gets his fresh fashion ideas.
“Fashion is a very versatile thing, it can be interpreted in many ways. And it holds so much importance because clothing is how many people are able to express themselves. Because of this, it is important that people are able to choose from an infinite selection of clothing to fill their wardrobe,” he said.
Like Marcus said, fashion is very important, and when we inquired about what his views are on local fashion stores, this is what he had to say.
“Local fashion is a way for fashion trends to establish themselves. Every community has its own unique fashion imprint and that’s what I think is so great about small clothing stores.” He said.
Well you heard the guy, supporting your local fashion stores has become all the more important and we should all start shopping locally. “Big corporations already profit so much from consumers that small businesses are crushed by their power.” Marcus added.
